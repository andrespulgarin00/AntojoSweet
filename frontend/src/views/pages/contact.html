Born from a passion for traditional French patisserie and modern flavor profiles, Antojo Sweet is more than a bakery—it's a sanctuary for the senses.

Our Origin

It started in a small kitchen with a single whisk and a family recipe for dark chocolate ganache. Our founder, Maria, envisioned a place where every bite told a story of quality, patience, and love. Today, Antojo Sweet brings that same artisanal dedication to every croissant, macaron, and bespoke cake we create.
